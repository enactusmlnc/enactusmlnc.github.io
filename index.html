About Us

Enactus MLNC is a student run social-entrepreneurial entity under the aegis of Enactus India. Currently, we are working on three projects: Project Parivartan- Under this project, we sell a range of handmade dog commodities manufactured by a group of underprivileged women residing in an urban slum in the capital of India, Delhi. The proceeds from our sales goes to both, our women entrepreneurs and the animal welfare NGO's associated with us. Project DESI- The acronym DESI stands for the Duty to Empathize, Sterilize and Immunize stray dogs. Under this project, we seek to bridge the gap between animal welfare NGO's and the Residents Welfare Associations of various localities, educational institutes and slums across India to serve a bilateral objective of eradicating rabies and controlling stray dog population that stands on the needs of both the stakeholders. Kindly navigate through the "PROJECTS" tab for further insight on our projects and the impact we've created through them. Under Project Sneh, cloth diapers are manufactured by a set of workers from Streebal NGO, for the children of indigent families. One of the most common misperceptions about cloth diapers is that they are difficult to use and maintain. Modern cloth diapers are just as easy to change and maintain as a disposable diaper. In addition to it, they have many benefits over disposables. They are eco-friendly, cost-effective, washable, absorbent, reusable, light and breathable.
