BeDesi Products

Home BeDesi

BeDesi collars are handmade collars made by underprivileged women residing at Delhi's Sanjay Gandhi Camp near Chanakyapuri. The profits generated from the sale of these collars primarily go to these women and a small portion is allocated for Municipality recognized NGOs who further use these funds to sterilize and vaccinate the stray dogs. BeDesi products are an outcome of Project Parivartan started by the students of Motilal Nehru college, University of Delhi, under the aegis of Enactus India. The project aims to empower women and help the stray dogs who live in the most deplorable conditions in India. So what are you waiting for, feel free to buy BeDesi collars and become a part of this wonderful initiative and help the stray dogs of our country.
