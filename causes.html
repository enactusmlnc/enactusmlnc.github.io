Media & Accolades

Home Media & Accolades

Zee Business

Enactus Motilal Nehru College was appraised by Zee business for Project DESI. They highlighted our initiative for one of the most ignored part of the ecosystem, the desi breed of dogs (stray dogs).

Dainik jagran

Enactus Motilal Nehru College was appraised by the national daily newspaper Dainik Jagran for the concept and implementation of the DESI model and the positive impact it created in the lives of domestic dogs.

The Asian Age

The Asian age recognized Enactus Motilal Nehru college as the only college society in India that is working towards animal welfare in terms of implementation of the DESI model and the awareness campaigns to promote the adoption of Indian dogs.

WoofBox

Pet blogging website woofbox.in joined hands with Enactus Motilal Nehru College as a marketer for the BeDesi products to support Project Parivartan.

DU Express

Enactus MNC launched a Campus Leadership Program which included students from all colleges of Delhi University. The main aim of this program was to make the youth aware of our duty, as a country, to look after our Desi Dogs. Many areas of Delhi NCR were included, where stray dogs were sterilized and vaccinated. This initiative was immensely supported by DU express, through an article about the program on its platforms.

BitGiving

Enactus Motilal Nehru College ran a successful crowd funding campaign by the name Humansforpawgress. The campaign was directed towards scaling our operations for the sterilization and immunization of stray dogs residing in various localities across India. The campaign was heavily supported by the human clan including acclaimed personalities such as Barkha singh (actress), Ranveer Allahbadia (youtuber), Arunoday Singh (poet and actor) and Manvi Gagroo (TVF model). The campaign generated a total funding of Rs.53831.

DU Beat

In 2015, Enactus MLNC joined hands with the PAWS, who helped us with the medications of the Desi breed. We paid them through proceeds of our handmade dog accessories. These articles were manufactured by the under privileged section, becoming a source of their employment. We were helped by DU Beat, which served us as a media platform for our work.

GARC

Enactus MNC conducted a Rabies Awareness Drive on World Rabies Day for school students and children residing in slum areas. The participants were made to complete the Rabies Educator Course, in order to make them familiar with the disease, its causes and consequences. We were helped by Global Alliance for Rabies Control, who not only provided us with the course, but also helped us in the promotion of the event.

Pet Fed

Acknowledging the magnitude and impact of our work to bring a positive change in the lives of the Indian stray dogs (the DESI breed), Pet Fed India regarded us with the Animal Welfare Award for the year 2017.

Youth ki Awaaz

An acclaimed online blogging portal “Youth ki Awaaz” recognized Enactus Motilal Nehru college for giving the stray dogs of Delhi a healthier life.

NAAC

Enactus MNC was termed as an “innovation” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council of India.

Enactus MNC won a cash prize of Rs. 1 lakh for winning the Campus Impact Challenge “LeadEARTHship” 2017, an initiative by TERI and Genpact.
